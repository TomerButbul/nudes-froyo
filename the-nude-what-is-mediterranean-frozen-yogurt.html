what is mediterranean frozen yogurt?

nudes · july 2026 · 4 min read

Frozen yogurt has had a lot of eras — the tart boom, the self-serve pump wall, the candy-topping arms race. Mediterranean frozen yogurt is something older and simpler. It goes back to the strained yogurts of the Levant and the Eastern Mediterranean, where yogurt is thick, tangy, and treated like real food. At nudes, that's the starting point for everything we make.

how it's different from regular froyo

Most American frozen yogurt is built for volume: lots of air, lots of sugar, and a thin base designed to run through a soft-serve machine. Mediterranean-style froyo flips the priorities. It starts from a strained, protein-rich yogurt — closer to Greek or Labneh in body — so the result is denser, creamier, and genuinely tangy instead of just sweet.

  • Thicker texture — less air whipped in, so every spoonful feels substantial.
  • Real tang — live cultures do the work, so you taste yogurt, not just sugar.
  • More protein — straining concentrates the protein naturally.
  • Cleaner label — fewer stabilizers and less refined sugar when it's done right.

why the mediterranean approach matters

In the Mediterranean, yogurt is rarely dessert-only. It shows up with honey and nuts, with fruit, with olive oil and sea salt. That mindset — treating yogurt as something craveable and nourishing — is exactly the gap we saw in Los Angeles. You shouldn't have to choose between something that tastes indulgent and something you actually feel good eating.

wellness, indulged. still doing the most, with less.

what to look for in a good cup

Whether you're at nudes or anywhere else, a few signals tell you the froyo is the real thing:

  1. It holds its shape and doesn't melt into soup in two minutes.
  2. The first taste is tangy, not just sweet.
  3. The ingredient list is short and readable.
  4. The toppings are things you'd actually keep in your kitchen — dates, pistachio, fig, honey, good chocolate.
